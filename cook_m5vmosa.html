Enjoy the crispy delight of Vada Pav and Samosa

Vada Pav

Ingredients:

  • 4 pav (bread rolls)
  • 4 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

For Batter:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for a thick batter)

Recipe:

  1. Heat oil, crackle mustard seeds, add curry leaves, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and mashed potatoes. Mix well. Add salt and coriander.
  2. Make small balls from the potato mixture.
  3. Prepare a thick batter using besan, turmeric, chili powder, salt, and water.
  4. Dip the potato balls in the batter and deep fry till golden.
  5. Slice pav, apply chutney, place the vada inside, and enjoy!

Samosa

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee or oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water as needed
  • 3 potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup green peas (boiled)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp dry mango powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger (grated)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Recipe:

  1. Mix flour, ghee, and salt. Knead with water into a stiff dough and rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and ginger. Sauté, then add mashed potatoes, peas, and all spices. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough, cut into halves, and shape into cones. Fill with stuffing and seal the edges.
  4. Deep fry on medium heat until golden brown. Serve hot with chutney.
