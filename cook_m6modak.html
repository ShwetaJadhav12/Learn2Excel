Celebrate with delightful Ukadiche Modak

Ukadiche Modak

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ghee
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 3/4 cup jaggery (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

Recipe:

  1. Boil water with salt and ghee. Add rice flour and stir. Cover and let it rest.
  2. For filling, cook coconut and jaggery till thick, then add cardamom powder.
  3. Knead the dough till smooth. Take small portions, shape into cups, and add filling.
  4. Seal edges, shape into modak, and steam for 10 minutes.
